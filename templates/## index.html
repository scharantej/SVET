Teach More About Siberia

Overview

Siberia is a vast and diverse region that occupies the northern part of Asia. It is home to a variety of landscapes, climates, and cultures. Siberia is also rich in natural resources, including oil, gas, and minerals.

Location

Siberia is located in the northern part of Asia. It is bordered by the Arctic Ocean to the north, the Ural Mountains to the west, the Pacific Ocean to the east, and Central Asia to the south.

Key Facts

  • Area: 13.1 million square kilometers (5.1 million square miles)
  • Population: 33.6 million (2020)
  • Capital: Novosibirsk
  • Largest city: Novosibirsk
  • Official languages: Russian, Buryat, Yakut, and various indigenous languages
  • Climate: Subarctic and continental
  • Natural resources: Oil, gas, minerals, timber, and furs
